How It Works

Working Principle

High-speed impact grinding with precision screen filtration and integrated dust control — delivering clean, consistent output every time.

01

High-Speed Impact

High-speed rotating hammers strike the material against wear-resistant liners inside the grinding chamber, shattering it into smaller particles.

02

Screen Filtration

A perforated screen at the bottom ensures only particles of the desired size pass through. Oversized material is retained for further grinding.

03

Positive Suction

A positive suction system at the outlet maintains proper airflow throughout, enabling a clean and completely dust-free operation.

The machine operates using high-speed rotating hammers that strike the material against wear-resistant liners inside the grinding chamber. A perforated screen ensures only the desired particle size passes through, while oversized material is retained for further grinding.

The positive suction system at the outlet maintains proper airflow — making it ideal for food, pharmaceutical, and chemical environments where clean operation is essential.
