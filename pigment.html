FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Which machine is suitable for pigment grinding?

Impact Pulverisers and Hammer Mills are commonly used depending on the required particle size and application.

Can abrasive pigments be processed?

Yes, machines can be equipped with wear-resistant grinding components suitable for abrasive materials.

Is particle size control possible?

Yes, machine selection and configuration can be customized to achieve the desired fineness.

Can pigments and additives be blended uniformly?

Yes, Ribbon Blenders provide homogeneous mixing for pigment formulations and additive blends.
